What do we do?

All aspects of building work including building maintenance, initial design and project management. We work on small or large projects ranging from private and domestic extensions to house and commercial builds as either a subcontractor or the main contractor.

About Us

We are a small family run business with 35 years of experience in the construction industry. We are located in and around the Guildford, Surrey area in the United Kingdom. Over the years we have gone from strength to strength.

Why choose us?

We have a conscientious approach and value attention to detail. We feel communication is key, quality is paramount and satisfaction is guaranteed. No matter if you're private or a main contractor we will work alongside you.
